Subzeruz

The Sabzeruz Festival is a festival in Sumeru that commemorates the birth of Lesser Lord Kusanali. The festival is named after the deceased Goddess of Flowers, who had initially conceived of the celebration to celebrate the birthday of her friend, the previous Dendro Archon Greater Lord Rukkhadevata.

Lantern Rite

Every year on the first day of the full moon, Li Yue celebrates the Sea Lantern Festival. At night, city residents release many sky lanterns and one large moon lantern into the sky. Inside each lantern there is a piece of plaustrite and the deepest desires written on special sticks. The lanterns slowly float into the distance, heralding the path for the souls of Li Yue’s faithful defenders.
